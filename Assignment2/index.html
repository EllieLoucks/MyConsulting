About Me

My name is Ellie Loucks from Massachusetts. I am one of five kids, not including my two dogs. I went to a very small high school so I had the opportunity to play many different sports since there were not enough kids to have cuts. My favorite sport was cheerleading which I did my junior and senior year. I also enjoyed being the soccer team manager also my junior and senior years. I thoroughly preferred this over playing soccer. I have also worked as a photo booth manager being hired to set up, work at, and pack up a portable photo booth at all different types of parties. Over the summer going into senior year I spent roughly 12 hours each day five days a week for 8 weeks as a camp counselor in Marblehead on an island. I would wake up at 5am then arrive to work around 7am to get home at roughly 6pm depending on the day. I am now a freshman at Miami University majoring in Emerging Technology in Business and Design. I am very excited for the next 4 years at Miami!
